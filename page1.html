Ashe, le kae? Ne ke batla gore...

Sometimes I sit quietly and think about how lucky I am to have met you — and every time, I’m filled with the same warmth, the same quiet joy, the same sense of gratitude. Even though we’ve only been together for a month, what I feel for you is already something steady, something meaningful, something that runs deeper than time alone can measure.

I’ve been trying to find the right words to tell you how much I appreciate you, but nothing I say feels quite enough to capture it all. Still, I want to try, because you deserve to know exactly how loved you are.

From the very beginning, you’ve had this calmness about you that makes my heart feel safe. You listen with intention. You speak with honesty. You love with care. There’s a softness in you that doesn’t try to take up all the space — instead, it makes room for me. For my thoughts, my emotions, my quirks, and even my insecurities. You let me show up fully as myself, and that is such a rare, beautiful gift.

Every conversation with you feels like a little home — a place where I can exhale, where I’m not trying to impress or explain too much. You just get me in a way that’s hard to put into words. And that connection? That comfort? It’s not something I take for granted. You bring peace to my world, Mathemba, and I hope you know that.
